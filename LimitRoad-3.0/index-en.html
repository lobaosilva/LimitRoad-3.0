About Us

About Us

We are a gaming community that, amidst so many ups and downs in gaming styles, has not let go of our passion for motorsport. A passionate and dedicated community in what they do. A community that is divided between forfun people who only play arcade games, racing games for laughs and trolling, oops. A bunch of guys who take it seriously, dedicate hours and hours in simulators looking for a perfect simulation, an incredible time and a lot of rivalry on the track. But in the end, they are all Limit Road, which end up in any game that has an old tin can, with an engine and four wheels for a good laugh.
